About the Fest

Novatos – In Detail

Novatos is the grand welcome ceremony for every new batch. It’s where you meet your people, show who you are, and start building your campus story.

What Happens Here

Novatos is curated for freshers to explore, connect and celebrate:

  • Mr & Ms Fresher runway & personality rounds
  • Solo & group talent performances
  • Interactive ice-breaking games & team tasks
  • Fun contests hosted by seniors & anchors
  • Photo booths, social corners, and live music

Why It Matters

First impressions, first friends, first stage moments — Novatos gives every fresher a safe, fun launchpad into college life.

Slogan: “Where new journeys begin, and new talents rise.”
