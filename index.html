About Exnovicta

Three signature fests. One powerful identity.

Exnovicta is the collective heartbeat of Medhavi Skill University’s cultural, literary and athletic spirit. It brings together three flagship experiences – Expression, Novatos and Invicta – each one unique, but all connected by one thing: the unstoppable energy of our students.

From open mics to open grounds, from freshers’ spotlights to fierce championships, Exnovicta transforms the campus into a universe of sound, lights, colours and stories. It’s not just a fest – it’s a tradition, a meeting point of talent and a memory-making machine that stays with every student long after the last track fades out.

Expression · Cultural & Literary Fest

Where culture speaks, and words paint worlds.

Expression is the space where stories walk, poems breathe and performances explode into life. It’s the cultural and literary soul of Exnovicta – a celebration of everything that makes art human.

At Expression, you’ll find poetry slams that give you goosebumps, dramatic monologues that silence an entire hall, stand-up acts that leave everyone in tears of laughter, and dance pieces that make time feel like it has slowed down. It’s a canvas for musicians, writers, actors, storytellers and experimental artists who want to say something real.

The fest also opens doors to panel discussions, informal adda sessions, book readings, theatre workshops and open mics under the stars. Expression is where shy first-years discover their voice, and seasoned performers find a new story to tell.

Invicta · Sports & Battle Fest

For the ones who refuse to back down.

Invicta is the roar of the crowd, the sweat on the field and the thrill of a close finish. It’s the festival of champions – for athletes, gamers and anyone who believes in the joy of competition.

From football, basketball and athletics to e-sports tournaments and strength challenges, Invicta brings out the fighter in every participant. It is where strategies are crafted, teams are formed, rivalries are born and legends are written on the scoreboard – sometimes in numbers, always in memories.

Under the stadium lights and in the digital arenas, Invicta is a reminder that winning is sweet, but playing with heart is unforgettable. Whether you’re cheering, coaching, playing or organising, Invicta makes sure you feel like part of something electric.

Novatos · Freshers' Fest

New faces. New dreams. An unforgettable welcome.

Novatos is the first big hello. It’s how the university tells every fresher, “You belong here.” It’s a festival built around warmth, fun and giving new students their first taste of campus magic.

From talent showcases and fun games to ramp walks, theme nights and freshers’ introductions, Novatos is the moment where strangers become classmates and classmates start becoming friends. It gives every newcomer a chance to step into the spotlight, share their story and start writing their own chapter at Medhavi Skill University.

The laughter, the nervous introductions, the inside jokes that start here – they turn into the long-term memories students look back on when they think of “how it all began.”
