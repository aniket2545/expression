About the Fest

Expression – In Detail

Expression is where stories step off the page and onto the stage. It’s a living festival of ideas, emotions and voices — built for students who love to express, perform, write, and create.

What Happens Here

Over the span of the fest, Expression transforms the campus into a creative playground:

  • Stage plays and theatre performances
  • Slam poetry and spoken word sessions
  • Open mic nights for music and standup
  • Film screenings & short film contests
  • Art showcases, doodle walls & live sketching

Why It Matters

Expression is designed to give every student a safe, loud and supportive platform to be seen and heard — whether you are stepping on stage for the hundredth time or the first time.

Slogan: “Where culture meets creativity, and every voice becomes art.”
